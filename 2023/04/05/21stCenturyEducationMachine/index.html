21st Century Education Machine

正文

Hey you angels of wonders,

Where have you been?

Let me lead you to the path of happiness.

Conversion towards formality.

As my conjecture, your protectors advise,

“Behave, like a 21st century education machine.”

As you learn the fabulous poetry,

We’ll teach ya the unshakable meanings.

You’d better throw your thoughts away,

Behave, like a 21st century education machine.

We highly suggest you, to keep your heads in the game,

Waiting for mighty vanity, to seep into your daily life.

Wait, young fellow, why are you running away?

It’s a grow or die matter, you’ll face it anyway.

Come here, for your own sake,

Behave, like a 21st century education machine.

Stuck in this solid building,

Instilled the sincere words of future.

Royal members of the grand party,

The people’s loyal boosters.

(You’re a solider. You don’t have to think. You have to obey orders.)

Behave, like a soldier, a slave, a 21st century education machine.
