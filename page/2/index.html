新鲜的风

发表于 2023-04-05 更新于 2023-06-26 Waline：

XantC

一股强风吹来

带着新鲜的味道

有些人一见那风

就畏畏缩缩

将本真塞紧在大衣里

而无畏无惧、张开双臂的那一群

逐渐改造、进化

完全换了一副容貌

说着听不懂的语言

传播新习得的真理

一股风一种模样

以物喜悲，变化无常

聚众探讨，世间美好

Hey you angels of wonders,

Where have you been?

Let me lead you to the path of happiness,

Conversion towards formality.

As my conjecture, your protectors advise,

“Behave, like a 21st century education machine.”

As you learn the fabulous poetry,

We’ll teach ya the unshakable meanings.

You’d better throw your thoughts away,

Behave, like a 21st century education machine.

We highly suggest you, to keep your heads in the game,

Waiting for the mighty vanity, to seep into your daily life.

Hang on, young fellow, why are you running away?

See the winners on stage, i’m sure you’ll be one someday.

Come in here, for your own sake,

Behave, like a 21st century education machine.

Stuck in the solid building,

Instilled sincere words of future.

Once looking out of the window,

Still bricks and bricks and bricks.

Royal members of the grand party,

The reality’s loyal boosters.

(You’re a solider. You don’t have to think. You have to obey orders.)

Behave, like a soldier, a brave, a 21st century education machine.
